About me

I grew up in Bremerton, Washington and graduated from Central Kitsap High School in 2019. I am a first generation college student and participated in Running Start in high school, earning an Associate's degree alongside my high school degree. I'm in the process of earning my Bachelors degree at UW Seattle. I have a strong passion for accessibility and diversity, and I have career goals of working somewhere in information technology.

Currently, I am working at UW-IT Academic Technologies as a Learning Technologies Consultant. I help the staff, students, and faculty of UW with tools like Canvas, Zoom, and Panopto through email, phone, and in-person consultations. Most of my duties include QA testing and helping provide solutions to encountered issues, often in a fast-paced environment.

Additionally, I am a paid writer for UW's student newspaper The Daily. I write for the Arts + Culture section, and run a column that reviews student and faculty member's favorite coming-of-age films. All of my articles can be read under the "Writing" tab.

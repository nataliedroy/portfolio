Environmental Data Project

This project was an assignment for INFO201 Spring 2020 quarter. The assignment was a group project to create a data visualization website revolving around a specific dataset.

Working with three other people, me and my team decided to focus on environmental data, specifically revolving around global warming and emissions. Using multiple datasets, we worked together to create three different interactive graphs.

This project was programmed in R and turned into a web app through Shiny. While we worked together on many aspects of the site, some of my main responsibilities were creating and designing the look of the website.

"Press A to Begin"

"Press A to Begin" is a 10 page zine I wrote and designed myself! After taking a film class that had a unit on the history and importance of zines in subcultures, I was inspired to make one myself digitally rather than using the more common handmade methods. This zine falls under the fanzine and personal zine genres and is about my love of video games and the experiences I've had around 10 specific video games.

The zine was designed and created using Adobe Photoshop during Winter 2020 over a few weeks. You can read it by clicking on the image above, or by clicking here.

Envision a Design Project

This project was an assignment for my INFO200 course in Winter 2020. The assignment was to design a non-commerical information system to solve some sort of issue people experience. Working with a team member, we had to design a solution to our issue, identify its benefits and harms, how information is exchanged within it, and decide how people interact with it.

Me and my teammate decided to focus on the issues people with dietary restrictions encounter while eating out in public. We designed an idea for an app titled Accomodate that would allow people to set reservations at restaurants with their eating accomodations and disabilities in mind.

The project was a written report that required visual aids, and along with writing many sections of the project, I was responsible for designing what the UI of the app would look like. The design was created by me in Adobe Photoshop.
